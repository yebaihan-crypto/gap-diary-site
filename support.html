技术支持与常见问题

常见问题 (FAQ)

如何创建并保存一条笔记？

在应用首页底部的“草稿纸”区域直接输入文字。写完后，点击上方任何一个带有分类名称的方块，笔记就会自动保存到该分类中。

如何查看某个分类下的历史笔记？

当底部的草稿纸为空时，点击任何一个分类方块，即可进入该分类的瀑布流视图，查看所有保存在此的笔记。

如何编辑或删除已有的笔记？

进入分类视图后，点击某张笔记卡片即可进行编辑。长按某张笔记卡片，会弹出“删除笔记”选项。

可以修改方块的名称吗？

可以。进入某个分类视图后，点击屏幕顶部的分类名称，即可输入新的名称进行修改。

我的数据会丢失吗？如何备份？

间隙日记不使用云端服务器，所有数据都保存在您的手机本地（极其安全且保护隐私）。为防止设备丢失或损坏导致数据丢失，请务必在手机设置中开启 iCloud 的「云备份」功能。

系统要求是什么？

间隙日记需要 iOS 17.0 或更高版本。

Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create and save a note?

Simply start typing in the "Draft Paper" area at the bottom of the main screen. When you're done, tap any category block above to instantly save the note into that category.

How do I view my past notes?

When the draft paper is empty, tap on any category block to enter its detail view and see all notes saved there in a waterfall layout.

How do I edit or delete a note?

Inside a category view, tap on any note card to edit it. Long-press on a note card to bring up the delete option.

Can I rename the categories?

Yes. Enter a category view and tap its name at the top of the screen to enter a new name.

How is my data backed up?

Gap Diary does not use external servers; all data is stored locally on your device for absolute privacy. To prevent data loss if you lose your device, please ensure iCloud Device Backup is enabled in your iOS settings.

What are the system requirements?

Gap Diary requires iOS 17.0 or later.
